Skill-Based Approaches to Education
Concept of Skill-Based Approaches to Education

Concept of Skill-based approaches to education Skill-based teaching is a process of developing efficiency in the fundamental components of teaching. It is a set of teacher behaviors that are especially effective in bringing about the desired change in learners.

Teaching is a skillful function. Without teaching skills, teachers can’t teach successively and effectively. It is necessary to merely use different skills for teaching learning activities by teachers. The use of different skills also helps the teacher with effective teaching and for students to learn simply. So, teaching is an approach to teaching students that are based on skills. Teaching is a skillful concept that changes according to the behaviors and social interests of learners as an individual. Skill-based teaching is a group of behaviors that especially bring expected changes in learners.

According to John Carol- “Skill-based teaching is a process of developing efficiency in the fundamental components of teaching.” Such a concept is developed through micro- teaching in real classes. There are three stages of skill development:

  1. Congnitive Phase: In this phase, we achieved different information about the content of skills
  2. Fixation Phase: In this phase, without stopping error response skills will be practiced frequently, or repeating functions will be done whenever skills will not be set up.
  3. Autonomous Phase : In this phase, more and more practices will be done on whether the speed of skill-based function will be strengthened as fast as possible

Skill is a mixture of simple and easiest habits of learners that are used carefully for their ideological objectives. Skill is that functional types of knowledge that are achieved through training and practices. Skill is such special activities or methodologies that are used in the classroom by teachers. It is the interaction with human behaviors. Skill is those functions that are fruitful for a long time in their life. To make teaching-learning activities effective in the classroom and develop special creative tasks for learning are called skills such skills are lectures, question answers, discussions, evaluation, feedback, etc.

Characteristics of SBE(Skill-based Education)
  1. Skills can be developed
  2. Skills can be measured
  3. Skills are applicable
  4. Skills are transferable
  5. Skills are efficient
Pillars of SBE(Skill-based Education)
  1. Learning to be
  2. Learning to know
  3. Learning to do
  4. Learning to live
